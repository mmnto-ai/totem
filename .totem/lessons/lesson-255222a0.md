## Lesson — Avoid refactoring synchronous factory functions to async

**Tags:** style, curated
**Pattern:** \basync\b.*\bimport\s*\(
**Engine:** regex
**Scope:** **/*.ts, **/*.js, **/*.tsx, **/*.jsx
**Severity:** warning

Avoid refactoring synchronous factory functions to async just to use dynamic import(). Node.js caches dynamic imports, so keep the factory synchronous to avoid unnecessary await boilerplate.
