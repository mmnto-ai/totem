## Lesson — When detecting Bun environments, check for both bun.lockb

**Tags:** architecture, curated
**Pattern:** ^(?=._\bbun\.lockb?\b)(?!._\bbun\.lockb\b._\bbun\.lock\b|._\bbun\.lock\b._\bbun\.lockb\b)._$
**Engine:** regex
**Scope:** **/\*.js, **/_.ts, \*\*/_.jsx, **/\*.tsx, **/_.sh, \*\*/_.yml, **/*.yaml, Dockerfile*
**Severity:\*\*\*\* error

When detecting Bun environments, check for both bun.lockb (legacy) and bun.lock (Bun >= 1.2) to ensure compatibility.
