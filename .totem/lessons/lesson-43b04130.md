## Lesson — Surface local fallbacks during initialization

**Tags:** ux, cli, ollama
**Scope:** packages/cli/src/commands/**/*.ts, !**/*.test.*, !**/*.spec.*

Proactively log the status of local model providers during setup to prevent cloud-key auto-detection from obscuring no-quota local alternatives. This maintains model-stack agnosticism by making the 'floor' visible to users before they configure cloud providers.
