## Lesson — Use Jaccard similarity for pattern coverage

**Tags:** algorithms, nlp, clustering
**Scope:** packages/core/**/*.ts, !**/*.test.*

A Jaccard similarity threshold of 0.6 on tokenized strings (stripping stopwords and short tokens) provides a reliable heuristic for identifying overlapping or covered patterns in text-based findings.
